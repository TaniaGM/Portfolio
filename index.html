About Me

I was born and raised in Canada. I am a member of the Gitxsan and Tsimshian Nations located in Northern British Columbia. My Gitxsan Nation is "Lu'al'al'yax. When translated into English it means, "Talking from the heart/talking from within." My brothers say it means, "Can't stop talking/Won't shut up."

My hobbies include traveling. This year we took a Trans-Atlantic cruise to Amsterdam. The most memorable stop was in Ireland where we rented a car and traveled to the Cliffs of Mohr for the day.
